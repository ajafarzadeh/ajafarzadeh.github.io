Ali Jafarzadeh is a PhD student in Finance at the Leeds School of Business, University of Colorado Boulder. His research interests span corporate finance, private equity, fintech, and cryptocurrency markets. Combining machine learning with financial econometrics, Ali explores innovative solutions to real-world financial problems.

Before joining CU Boulder, Ali completed his M.S. in Economics at Tarbiat Modares University and a B.S. in Engineering at the University of Tehran. His diverse academic background provides a unique perspective on empirical finance research.
