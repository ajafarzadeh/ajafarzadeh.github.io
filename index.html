Ali Jafarzadeh

Finance PhD Student

Leeds School of Business

Ali Jafarzadeh is a PhD student in Finance at the Leeds School of Business, University of Colorado Boulder. His research focuses on empirical corporate finance, private equity, machine learning, and cryptocurrency markets. Ali's work aims to bridge the gap between data science and financial decision-making, contributing innovative insights to the field of finance.

Before joining CU Boulder, Ali earned an M.S. in Economics with a minor in Mathematics from Tarbiat Modares University and a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering (specializing in Food Machinery) from the University of Tehran. Alongside his academic pursuits, Ali played competitive volleyball for the University of Tehran during his undergraduate years, showcasing his dedication both on and off the court.

Beyond academia, Ali is deeply passionate about Persian music and plays the Ney, the traditional Iranian flute. He enjoys hiking, exploring the outdoors, and spending quality time with family and friends, balancing his professional ambitions with personal enrichment and connection.
